I came to Dev Bootcamp looking to embark on a new career in software, but my interests extend beyond technology. After graduating from the University of Kansas in 2006 with a BA in History, I spent four+ years working on a PhD at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign learning about world history and studying foreign languages while occassionally dabbling in obscure Arabic and Turkish translation projects and obsessively reading social theory. I remain especially interested in present day Turkish soap opera depictions of the golden age of Ottoman history. My other passions include basketball, sports in general, the outdoors, Kansas City barbecue, computer and other hardware, and YouTube. If I were trapped on an island and could only choose one book, it would be The Protestant Ethic and the Spirit of Capitalism.
