About Me

I came to Dev Bootcamp looking to embark on a new career in software development, but my interests extend beyond tech. After graduating from the University of Kansas in 2006 with a BA in History, I spent four+ years working on a PhD at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign learning about Middle Eastern and world history, studying Arabic and Turkish, and reading social theory. I remain especially interested in Ottoman history.

Outside of DBC I am passionate about books, foreign languages, basketball, Kansas City barbecue, computer software and hardware, and YouTube.
