I came to Dev Bootcamp to nourish my interest in software design and to deepen my understanding of coding languages and practices that drive web applications and other programs. Prior to DBC, I worked as a computer tech and system administrator, and was planning on moving into more of a network engineering role, but ultimately I found that my passion lies in application development. Before entering the tech field, I graduated from the University of Kansas with a BA in History and spent four years in a History PhD program at the University of Illinois, where I studied Arabic and Turkish on scholarships from the US Department of Education.
